Our Company

ParadiseRealestate.com is the world's largest condo marketplace. The online real estate portal was launched in 2006 to connect buyers and sellers and to create liquidity in the growing global condo market. The site was developed by eRealEstate Holdings, an internet marketing company that also owns and operates Houses.com and Property.com. The site showcases over 5 million properties for sale, rent and vacation in the United States and 70+ countries around the world. In addition to property for sale and rent, site visitors have access to a wide variety of real estate-related products and services including mortgages, credit repair, home improvement, moving and more. The site receives over 1 million visitors each month and cost-effectively delivers exposure and qualified leads to builders, real estate professionals, service providers and homeowners. The Company is privately held and headquartered in Miami, Florida. For more information, please contact us here.
